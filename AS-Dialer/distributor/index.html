Distributor

Distributors sell Dialer360 under their own brand. No mention is made of the Dialer360 brand, so a Distributor is responsible for their own marketing, sales and training materials.

  • You set your own price.
  • Optionally bundle Dialer360 with your other services, e.g. hosting and termination.
  • Branded demo system provided.
  • Pre-sales support provided to you.
  • You provide training and first line support.
  • We provide installation services.
  • 2nd and 3rd line technical support provided via you.
  • Customer pays you.
To qualify as a distributor, we will require your business and sales plan.

Please contact us for more details on how to become a Dialer360 Distributor.
